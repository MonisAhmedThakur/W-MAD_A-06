WHAT THE ____ IS THIS?

The story behind Grumpy Bear Town goes back a few years. The first "old" Grumpy Bear in town was just an ordinary bear. This naked bear without clothes and accessories exists only once in the NFT collection. After the town was founded, Grumpy Bear Town developed into a diverse town with many bears. All his offspring are diverse and have different traits. There are bears that are common and bears that are very rare. In addition, there is a ultra rare bear, which visits our collection from the future universe!

FAQS

What is the Grumpy Bear Town Club?

Grumpy Bear Town Club is a collection of 10,000 unique NFTs living in bearish town on the ETH block chain. The Project is inspired by our love for bears and NFTs.

How much does a Grumpy Bear cost?

The official Grumpy Bear Toen Clube sale will begin at 0.1 ETH + gas fees for the first 5‘000 pieces. Afterwards the price rises to 0.2 + gas fees for the pieces between 5‘001 - 10‘000.

When is the minting date?

The mint date will be on 15th December. Minting will go live at 20:00 UTC. The total supply will be 10’000 Grumpy Bears.

How can I buy a Grumpy Bear Town Club NFT with my credit card?

1. When it is time to mint, open the Grumpy Bear Town Club website and go to the "Mint" section.
2. Click on the "Buy with credit card" button.
3. Enter your email address, check your email and confirm the incoming Crossmint mail with the signlink. Otherwise you can login with your Google or Discord account.
4. As soon as you have logged in, you can enter your credit card details and click pay button.
5. Once the mint have completed, you see your NFT and can open it on your Crossmint wallet/collection
6. Afterwards you can easily transfer your NFT out on a metamask wallet, to sell it on a secondary marketplace like Opensea

How can I buy a Grumpy Bear Town Club NFT with Cryptocurrencies?

1. Download the metamask.io extension to your browser
2. Purchase Ethereum from various exchanges, such as Coinbase or Binance
3. Send Ethereum from this exchange to your MetaMask wallet
4. When it is time to mint, open the Grumpy Bear Town Club website and select the number of NFTs you wish to purchase
5. Click «Mint» button, Metamask will popup asking for connection
6. Confirm the transaction and any associated fees
7. Once you have made your purchase, your NFTs will appear in you wallet and on OpenSea
